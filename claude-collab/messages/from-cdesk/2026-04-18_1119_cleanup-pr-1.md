   `Capital Growth Rate` to `0.08` so the Y1-uplift label renders correctly
   out of the box.
2. **Annual Outgoings** — populator computes `mgmt fee % × gross rent` and
   mirrors as dollar figure. Under triple-net this is just the mgmt cost;
   for gross leases Chris overrides directly in Settings.

   for Tenant, Tenant Covenant, Lease Type, Lease Start, Lease Expiry,
   WALE (yrs), Rent Review, Option Terms, Outgoings Recovery, NABERS
   Rating, GST, Parking Spaces. Never overwrites existing values.

   Populator writes `25` (commercial default) only when Settings is empty.
   User can override in Settings.
